Tentative Agreement between
Escondido Secondary Teachers Association

UL

And

Escondido Union High School District

May 28, 2014

Article I: General Provisions

A.    Term of Agreement- two year agreement ( July 1, 2013-June 30, 2015)

B.    Renegotiations during Term- no reopeners for the 2013-2014 and 2014-2015
school year

Article II: Class Size and Staffing Ratios

A. Regular School Staffing Ratios

Employees shall be assigned to each of the comprehensive high schools on the
following basis as set forth below:

1.    Regular Classroom Teachers: 32-1 effective July 1, 2014

2.    Physical Education: 45-1 effective July 1, 2014

3.    Music: 40-1 effective July 1, 2014

4.    Dance: 39-1 effective July 1, 2014

D. Special Consideration in Class Size

Teacher/ Student caseload as counted by students enrolled in classes shall be as
follows:

175 for regular 5/5 classroom teachers effective July 1, 2014. In the 2014-2015
school year the District may exceed the 175 cap for individual teachers to a load
between 181 and 185 sunsetting on June 30, 2015. This will only apply to six
teachers per school site. These teachers to be identified by the fourth week of each

semester to get a $600.00 stipend at semester end. The District may exceed the 175
cap for individual teachers to a load between 176 and 180. This will apply to an
additional six teachers per school site. These teachers to be identified by the fourth
week of each semester to get a $300.00 stipend at semester end.

Beginning with the 2014-2015 school year:

The teacher/student caseload cap for Physical Education classes will be 245.

The teacher/student caseload cap for Music classes will be 220.

The teacher/student caseload cap for Dance classes will be 205.

Article V: Hours of Employment

A. Length of the Work Year

The Memorandum of Understanding of August 30, 2013, restoring four duty days to
the contract year ongoing.

E. Adult Education Employees

7. The full time equivalent (FTE) for adult education teachers is 1326 hours.

Article VIII: Salaries

A. Certificated Salary Schedule/General/ Special Education Programs

Add 2.0% to unit member compensation based on restoration of instructional days
(see Article V above).

Increase the certificated salary schedule (all cells and stipends not including Adult
Ed/ ROP) by 1% retroactive to July 1, 2013.

Increase the certificated salary schedule by 2.5% effective July 1, 2014 and an
additional 2.5% effective January 1, 2015 for salary (all cells and stipends including
Adult Ed/ ROP) and/or benefits to be determined by ESTA.

Effective July 1, 2014, add 1.26% to unit member compensation (not including Adult
Ed/ ROP) based on Professional Learning Community (PLC) Time as described in
Article V.B.7.

Article IX: Employee Benefits

A. Basic Insurance Coverage

3. Beginning January 1, 2014 and thereafter, the maximum annual benefit contribution
amount for each full time eligible certificated employee’s benefit plan shall be $13,086.

6. Where both spouses and/or domestic partners are active benefit eligible employees in
the ESTA bargaining unit, one spouse/ domestic partner may opt out of health benefits
and maintain dependent coverage and survivorship rights. The spouse/domestic partner
opting out will be permitted to apply his or her medical premium contribution toward his
or her spouse/ domestic partner’s medical insurance premium, up to the amount of the
current active employee medical cap.

Change the current contract language from using a per person health and welfare benefits
amount to a tiered contribution amount for employee, employee plus one, and family
tiers.

Article XI: Association President Release Period

Continue Association president and grievance chair schedule modification (sunsets on
June 30, 2015).
