The 2012-2013 salary schedule, including hourly pay and stipends, shall be increased by 3% retroactive to July 1,2013.

The 2013-2014 salary schedule, including hourly pay and stipends, shall be increased by an additional 2% beginning July 1,2014.

negotiations team.
