ARTICLE I: AGREEMENT

LI

The over-riding purpose of this written agreement is to achieve the type of working
relationship between the School District and the certificated staff which will, within the
resources available, allow the maximum possible educational benefit to the students.

1.2

It is the purpose of this bilateral agreement to set forth the full, complete and entire
understanding of the parties reached as a result of meeting and negotiating regarding
wages, hours and other terms and conditions of employment of the employees covered
hereby.

1.3

This document shall, upon the signed approval of the governing board representative of
the District and the authorized representative of the Association, become a binding labor
agreement as set forth by applicable California Government Code.
