DEFINITION OF TERMS

Definitions

1.1.1    "Act" means Chapter 10.7, Sections 3540 through 3549.3 of Division 4
of Title 1 of Government Code of the State of California.

1.1.2    'Association*' means the Jamul-Dulzura United Teachers Association/
CTA/NEA.

1.1.3    "Board" as used herein is the Board of Trustees of the Jamul-Dulzura
Union School District.

1.1.4    "Certificated Employee" as used herein means a Member of the Unit.

1.1.5    "Member of the Unit” refers to any certificated employee of the District
covered by this Agreement.

1.1.6    "Regular, Full-Time Employee" is defined as one who is assigned
under contract for one semester or more to work the entire day for all
of the duty days as provided in this Agreement.

1.1.7    "Part-Time Employee" is defined as one who is assigned under
contract for one semester or more to work less than the regular full¬
time employee as defined in this Agreement. (Sharing of a teaching
assignment is addressed in board policy.)

1.1.8    "Calendar Year" refers to the yearly period from January 1 to
December 31.

1.1.9    "Days" are calendar days.

1.1.10    "Duty Days" are days on which the Members of the Unit are required to
be in attendance.

1.1.11    "School Days" are days in which students are required to be in
attendance.

1.1.12    "Instructional Day" refers to the daily amount of in-class time required

RECOGNITION

Recognition

2.1.1    The Board hereby recognizes the Association as the exclusive negotiating
representative of the Members of the Unit, which includes: temporary,
probationary, or permanent part-time or full-time teachers. Members of
the Unit shall specifically include classroom teachers, Special Education
teachers, Language Speech & Hearing specialists, Categorical
Instructional Support teachers, and Certificated Curriculum Experts. The
following certificated positions are excluded from the bargaining unit:
Superintendent, Principal, Assistant Principal, School Psychologist, and
Substitute Teachers.

2.1.2    No other group, organization, individual or representative thereof shall be
permitted to engage in any meeting and negotiating with the District on
behalf of any employee included in the Unit.

HOURS OF EMPLOYMENT

Unit Member(s) Work Year

5.1.1    The number of duty days of Members of the Unit shall be one hundred
eighty-six (186) as identified in the school calendar.

This shall include:

--180 school days
- 6 faculty work days

5.1.1.1    Prior to the start of the student instructional year, one (1) full

faculty work day, plus the equivalent of not less than one (1)
additional full faculty work day shall be designated for teacher
directed room setup/planning time. The District shall consult
with JDUTA regarding the calendaring and agenda for the
remaining faculty work days.

5.1.2    Should a school day be canceled for any reason, including rainy day, fire,
etc., the same amount of time will be added to the school year so that the
minimum of 180 school days will be maintained.

perform teaching-related duties which include, but are not limited to

District and local school curriculum development, educational field trips,

teacher conferences. Teachers of Grades 6-8 at Oak Grove Middle
